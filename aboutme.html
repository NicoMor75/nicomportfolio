I'm Nico...

I am a graphic designer that takes simple shapes and turns them into great designs. Throughout my years as a designer, I have covered essentially all areas of the discipline, from video editing and animation, to typography, illustration and concept design. Especially with my posterwork, I use typography and spacing to give a podium to the subject matter at hand. In my concept designs, I allow symbolism to build identity and distinction. I take inspiration from many sources, but large influences include designers like Emily Oberman and Milton Glaser. In my illustrations, I take inspiration from media like Avatar the Last Airbender, Lord of the Rings, and Dungeons and Dragons. I have done work in Northeast Ohio for years but also take clients from all over the US.
